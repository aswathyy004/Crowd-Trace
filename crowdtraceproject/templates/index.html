About

we are dedicated to leveraging technology and community collaboration to aid in the urgent search for missing individuals. Our platform provides a vital connection between citizens, law enforcement, and administrators, working together to bring loved ones home.

WHO WE ARE

Building bridges between families,communities and vital resources

CrowdTrace was born from a deep understanding of the challenges faced in missing persons cases. We recognized the critical need for a centralized, accessible, and collaborative platform to bridge the gaps in information sharing and accelerate search efforts. Our commitment is driven by the belief that every missing person deserves to be found, and every family deserves answers.
